The players
The Activist

Activists go about shouting and waving their arms about until things happen. When that isn't effective, they start dancing around and wear funny coloured hats. Finally, when they get an audience, then suddenly stop and say, "What? What's the matter? Why are you looking at me?"

The Magician

A magician is a master of illusion and slight-of-hand. They keep a small reserve of pixie dust in their back pocket and sprinkle it upon the objects they are doing their magic on. Then they say strange and incomprehensible things to sway people to think that they are casting a spell when really it's just a distraction while their assistant quietly steals people's wallets, watches and house keys.
